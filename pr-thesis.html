Mandala Endangered Species Conservation Park

For my illustration thesis at Ringling College of Art and Design, I decided to take on a project that would combine two of my biggest interests: themed entertainment and zoology. The images on this page are my final concepts and designs for the fictional zoo/theme park hybrid I called Mandala; they include illustrations of the animals housed within the park, concept art of guests in the park on opening day, a souvenir map of the grounds, sample merchandise, etc.
